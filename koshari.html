How to make Koshari At Home (:

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Pasta (macaroni or any short pasta)
  • Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. Boil lentils until tender, then drain excess water.
  3. Cook pasta according to package instructions, then set aside.
  4. In a separate pot, boil chickpeas until soft.
  5. In a pan, saut√© chopped onions and minced garlic in cooking oil until golden brown.
  6. Add tomato sauce to the onion and garlic mixture, simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Season the tomato sauce with salt, pepper, and a splash of vinegar to taste.
  8. Mix the cooked lentils, rice, pasta, and chickpeas in a large bowl.
  9. Pour the tomato sauce mixture over the rice and lentil mixture.
  10. Toss everything together until well combined.
  11. Serve the Koshari hot, optionally garnished with fried onions on top.
